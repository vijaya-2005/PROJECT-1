A shoe is an item of footwear intended to protect and comfort the human foot . It is usually made of leather with a stiff or thick sole and heel, and generally reaches no higher than the ankle. Shoes are designed to cover, protect, and comfort the human feet, and are also used as decoration and fashion. Footwears protect the feet and give stability to the body.
